# Code of conduct

## Standard

This project follows the Contributor Covenant. Participants are expected to act with respect.
Harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory language are not tolerated.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces, including the issue tracker, pull requests, and any other
channel used for project communication, and to public spaces when an individual is representing
the project.

## Expected behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the project.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment of any participant.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Reports of unacceptable behavior may be sent to the repository owner through the contact listed on
the repository. The owner reviews reports and responds as warranted, up to and including removing a
participant from project spaces.
