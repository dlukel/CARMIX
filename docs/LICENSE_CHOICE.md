# License choice

CARMIX is released under the GNU Affero General Public License version 3 (AGPL-3.0). See the LICENSE
file in the repository root.

## Why AGPL-3.0

AGPL-3.0 is a strong copyleft license. Anyone who distributes the software, or runs a modified
version to provide a service over a network, must make the corresponding source available under the
same terms. The network-use clause is the reason for choosing it. It closes the hosted-service gap
that a plain GPL leaves open, so a third party cannot take CARMIX, modify it, and offer it as a
closed network service. Open stays open. The priority here is keeping the project and its
derivatives free.

## The alternative

If the priority later shifts to maximum adoption over keeping derivatives open, a permissive license
such as BSD or MIT would be the alternative. That trades the copyleft guarantee for the widest
possible reuse, including in closed products. It is the owner's call if the goals change.

## Contributor agreement

Requiring a contributor license agreement or a developer certificate of origin from contributors
preserves the owner's right to relicense later. CONTRIBUTING.md notes this.
